FAQ

Questions, answered.

Your commission is based on the original product price multiplied by your tier rate (20%, 30%, or 40%). If you've given a discount coupon to the buyer, that discount amount is deducted from your commission. For example: if a product is ₹500, you're a Standard affiliate (20%), and your coupon gives 10% off — your commission = (₹500 × 20%) − (₹500 × 10%) = ₹100 − ₹50 = ₹50.
Upgrades are based on your cumulative sales count. You'll be upgraded to Growth Partner (30% commission) once you hit 25 total sales, and to Elite Partner (40% commission) at 100 total sales. Your dashboard shows your current progress toward the next tier.
Percentage discounts reduce the product price by a % (e.g., 15% off). Fixed discounts reduce by a flat INR amount (e.g., ₹200 off). Fixed discount coupons are only available to Growth and Elite partners, with a maximum fixed discount of ₹498. Both types of discounts are deducted from your commission earnings.
Your affiliate link format is: creatorstack.breakfastclub.co.in/PRODUCT_ID?ref=YOUR_ID. When someone clicks your link and makes a paid purchase, the sale is automatically attributed to your account. You'll see it reflected in your dashboard instantly.
Yes! You can create multiple unique coupon codes from your dashboard, each with different discount rates and validity settings. Your dashboard even shows how many times each coupon has been used, so you can spot which codes perform best with your audience.
From your dashboard, go to the Payouts section and click "Request Payout." Enter the amount you'd like to withdraw. Our team processes payouts to the Indian bank account you registered with. Typical processing time is 3–5 business days.
When creating a coupon, you choose the validity type: either a fixed number of sales (e.g., first 50 uses) or a fixed number of days (e.g., valid for 30 days). Once the limit is reached, the coupon automatically becomes inactive.
Anyone with an Indian bank account can apply — students, freelancers, creators, self-employed individuals, or side-hustlers. Just fill out the onboarding form, verify your email via OTP, and you're in. All affiliates start at the Standard Partner tier.
Sales are tracked in real-time. The moment a buyer completes a paid order using your link or coupon, it appears in your dashboard. Commission is calculated immediately and added to your pending revenue balance.
Elite affiliates achieving 100 or more monthly sales are enrolled in our exclusive rewards program. Rewards include international travel experiences, premium electronics, accessories, and more — all fully sponsored by CreatorStack. Gifts are dispatched without warning, so just keep an eye on your inbox! 🎁
